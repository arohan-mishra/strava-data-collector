What data will be collected?

  • Running activity distance, time, pace, elevation and date
  • Heart rate and cadence if available from Strava
  • Basic Strava profile fields such as athlete ID and general location

What will not be collected?

  • Exact GPS route coordinates are not saved in this app
  • Strava access tokens are not saved to CSV
  • Your data is used only for this assignment/project demonstration
