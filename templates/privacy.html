Privacy and Consent

By connecting your Strava account, you allow this prototype to read your activity data and save only selected running activity fields for analysis.

Purpose

The data will be used to demonstrate the feasibility of a data science project focused on recreational runner injury risk prevention.

Data minimisation

The app saves activity summary fields such as distance, moving time, elevation, start date, speed, heart rate and cadence where available. It does not save exact GPS coordinate arrays.

Voluntary participation

Participation is voluntary. You can stop before authorising Strava access.
