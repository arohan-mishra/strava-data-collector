Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This privacy policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected within the Recreational Runner Research Platform.

1. Purpose of the Application

This application is an educational and research project developed as part of a university data science assignment focused on injury risk prediction in recreational runners using wearable activity data.

2. Data Collected

With user consent through Strava OAuth authentication, the application may collect:

  • Running activity distance
  • Moving time and elapsed time
  • Average pace and speed
  • Elevation gain
  • Heart rate data (if available)
  • Cadence data
  • Activity timestamps
  • General activity metadata provided by Strava

The application does NOT intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as:

  • Passwords
  • Financial information
  • Private messages
  • Exact residential addresses

3. How the Data Is Used

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Academic research and coursework
  • Exploratory data analysis
  • Development of injury risk prediction models
  • Statistical analysis and visualization
  • Educational demonstrations related to data science

4. Data Storage

Data may be stored locally or on secure cloud infrastructure for research purposes. Reasonable efforts are made to protect collected data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

Data collected through this application will not be sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Aggregated or anonymized analysis results may be included in academic reports or presentations.

6. User Consent

Users voluntarily authorize access to their Strava activity data through the official Strava OAuth authorization process.

Users may revoke access to the application at any time through their Strava account settings.

7. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Strava API and complies with Strava’s API usage policies.
